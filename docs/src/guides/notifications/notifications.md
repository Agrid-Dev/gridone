# Notifications

## Background

A [notification](../../reference/glossary.md#notification) is a message dispatched to one or more users about an event — for example, when a [device](../../reference/glossary.md#device) turns [faulty](../../reference/glossary.md#fault) or when an [automation](../../reference/glossary.md#automation) fires.

---

## Notifications page

Click the bell icon in the top bar to see all notifications for your account. A red dot on the icon indicates unread notifications.

On the Notifications page, use the filters at the top of the page to narrow the list based on:

- **Severity** — show only notifications of a given [severity](../../reference/glossary.md#severity).
- **Status** — show **All**, **Unread** only, or **Dismissed** only.

Each row shows the notification title and message, its severity, when it was received, and when it was dismissed (if applicable).

---

## Dismiss a notification

**Single** — click **Mark as read** on the row. The notification is marked dismissed immediately.

**Bulk** — check the boxes next to the notifications you want to dismiss, then click **Mark as read (N)** in the page header.

> Already-dismissed notifications appear faded in the list.
